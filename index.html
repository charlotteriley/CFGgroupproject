About Us

The Pigeon Protection League was established in 2022. Our mission is to take all of the UK's unloved pigeons under our wing. From many a town square to a neglected underpass, hundreds of thousands of these birds find themselves going hungry and without a warm nest for the night. But did you know that pigeons make wonderful pets? And in return for some bird food and a snuggly nest, they will shower you with affection.

We at the Pigeon Protection League aim to unite YOU with the neglected pigeon of your dreams. Get in touch with us today and start preparing your home, and your life, for your adorable new pet.
